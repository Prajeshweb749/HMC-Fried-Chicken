Fried Chicken

A beloved dish, is made by coating chicken pieces in seasoned flour and frying them until golden and crispy. This delightful meal is celebrated for its juicy, tender meat and crunchy exterior, offering a savory experience that brings comfort and satisfaction to all who indulge.

Chicken Noodles

A popular dish, combine tender chicken pieces with flavorful noodles, stir-fried to perfection. Infused with a mix of savory sauces and fresh vegetables, this comforting meal offers a delightful blend of textures and tastes, making it a favorite for many food enthusiasts.

Chicken Momos

A delectable delicacy, feature seasoned chicken filling wrapped in soft dough, then steamed or fried. These bite-sized delights are enjoyed with spicy dipping sauces, offering a burst of flavors and a satisfying texture, making them a cherished favorite among many food lovers.
